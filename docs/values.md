# Values — CoAI Network

1. **Human-centered** — People and their rights come first.
2. **Transparency** — Open and verifiable practices.
3. **Scientific rigor** — Evidence-based discourse and decisions.
4. **Inclusion** — Diverse voices are welcome and encouraged.
5. **Ethical precaution** — Safety and well-being are a priority.
6. **Collaboration** — Bridges between academia, industry, and society.
